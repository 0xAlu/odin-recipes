Mexican Tostadas

Authentic Mexican Tostadas in Minutes!

Make some of the best, authentic Mexican tostadas at home in less than 10 minutes! Craving restaurant-style tostadas? Try our recipe to wow everyone for lunch/dinner!

Ingredients

  • Tostadas
  • Black refried beans
  • Queso ranchero
  • Avocado
  • Red/Green Salsa
  1. Start by spreading the black refried beans over a tostada.
  2. Crumble the cheese on top of the beans.
  3. Prepare the avocado in slices and apply generously on top.
  4. Drizzle your salsa of choice to finish.
