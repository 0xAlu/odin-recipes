Simple Date-Night Antipasto

Try our recipe for a simple antipasto perfect for date night!

Looking to plan an easy stay-at-home date night? Look for no further! Try assembling our simple-to-make charcuterie that can be bought separately at your local supermarket! Featuring meats, cheeses, fruits, crackers, and spreads!

Ingredients

  • Charcuterie meat variety pack
  • Smoked cheese variety pack
  • Ritz crackers or your favorite brand's variation
  • Green grapes
  • Strawberries
  • Mesquite honey
  • Bonus: pick up a bottle of your favorite wine!
  1. In a large plate, place meats, cheeses, and fruits.
  2. In a bowl, place crackers in an aesthetic manner.
  3. Open your bottle of wine and pour 2 glasses.
  4. That's it! Enjoy!
